Being raised in the midwest by a father who worked in a factory his whole life, I want to bring that same ethic to my work through meaningful and thoughtful design. Building digital products is what I do and working with The Scenery I am learning to lead and teach — the same way all my mentors have guided me.

Like my father, I love bringing the energy to the party and laughter to everyone. In the wise words of Fetty Wap, “I just live every day like I ain’t gonna live the next one.”

I’m Christian, and that’s just what I am going to do.
